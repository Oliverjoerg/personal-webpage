Welcome to my reading side

Here you will find some of my favorite books that I loved to read.

Robert Jordan - Das Rad der Zeit

A fantasy book series that is considered one of the greatest in the genre, known for its complex story and world-building.

Terry Goodkind - Das Schwert der Wahrheit

A captivating series that blends fantasy and philosophy, with a gripping narrative and deep characters.

Christopher Paolini - Eragon

A high-fantasy series that follows the journey of a young dragon rider and his quest to defeat an evil king.

Richard Schwartz - Die Getter Kriege Saga

An exciting series filled with adventure, magic, and intriguing characters.
