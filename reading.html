Welcome to my reading page

Here you will find some of my favorite books that I loved to read.

Robert Jordan - Das Rad der Zeit

A fantasy book series that is considered one of the greatest in the genre, known for its complex story and world-building.

Terry Goodkind - Das Schwert der Wahrheit

A captivating series that blends fantasy and philosophy, with a gripping narrative and deep characters.

Christopher Paolini - Eragon

A high-fantasy series that follows the journey of a young dragon rider and his quest to defeat an evil king.

Richard Schwartz - Die Götter Kriege Saga

An exciting series filled with adventure, magic, and intriguing characters.

J.K. Rowling - Harry Potter Series

A beloved fantasy series that chronicles the life and adventures of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his fight against the dark wizard Voldemort.

Christina Henry - The Chronicles of Alice Series

A dark and twisted retelling of the classic Alice in Wonderland story, filled with adventure and suspense.

J.R.R. Tolkien - The Hobbit

A timeless fantasy tale about the journey of Bilbo Baggins, who embarks on an adventure to help a group of dwarves reclaim their homeland.

John Flanagan - Die Chroniken von Araluen

An engaging series that follows the adventures of an apprentice Ranger named Will, set in a richly detailed medieval world.
