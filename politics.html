Welcome to my politics page

Not only am I deeply interested in politics, but I also study it extensively. I have a keen interest and substantial knowledge in conflict and security, comparative politics, international relations, European Union integration, climate change and environmental policy, governance, and public policy.

Conflict and Security

Understanding the dynamics of conflicts and the measures required for security, including both national and international perspectives.

Comparative Politics

Comparing political systems, institutions, and practices across different countries to understand the diversity and commonality in political life.

International Relations

Understanding the relationships between countries, the role of international organizations, and the impact of global events on local and international politics.

European Union Integration

Studying the process and impact of European Union integration on member states and the broader international community.

Climate Change and Environmental Policy

Analyzing the political responses to climate change and the development and implementation of environmental policies.

Governance

Examining how various forms of governance impact political stability, efficiency, and public trust in political institutions.

Public Policy

Analyzing the creation and implementation of public policies, and their effects on society and various population groups.
