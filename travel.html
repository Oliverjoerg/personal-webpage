Travel

Welcome to my travel page

Here you will find some highlights and memorable experiences from the countries I have visited.

North America

Explored the vibrant cities of New York and Los Angeles, enjoyed the natural beauty of the Grand Canyon, and experienced the cultural diversity across different states.

Peru

Visited the interesting and beautiful city of Lima.

Japan

Enjoyed the bustling streets of Tokyo, the serene temples of Kyoto, and the beautiful cherry blossoms during spring.

China

Explored the Great Wall, experienced the vibrant life in Beijing and Shanghai.

Norway

Marveled at the stunning fjords, enjoyed the midnight sun in the summer, and visited the charming city of Oslo.

Denmark

Explored the historic streets of Copenhagen, visited the famous Tivoli Gardens, and enjoyed the Danish "hygge" culture.

Germany

Visited the beautiful cities of Düsseldorf and Hamburg.

Austria

Experienced the cultural richness of Vienna, skied in the Alps, and enjoyed the scenic beauty of Salzburg.

Morocco

Explored the vibrant markets of Marrakech, and experienced the astonishing Sahara Desert.

France

Visited the iconic Eiffel Tower in Paris, enjoyed the wine regions of Bordeaux and Burgundy, and relaxed on the French Riviera.

Spain

Experienced the lively culture of Barcelona, the historic sites of Madrid, and the beautiful city of San Sebastian.

Italy

Explored the ancient ruins of Rome, enjoyed the vibrant city of Milano.

Hungary

Visited the historic city of Budapest, and experienced the rich cultural heritage.

Turkey

Explored the vibrant city of Istanbul, and enjoyed the beaches of Antalya.

Dominican Republic

Relaxed on the beautiful beaches of Punta Cana, and enjoyed the vibrant local culture.

Iceland

Marveled at the stunning waterfalls and geysers, soaked in the Blue Lagoon, and experienced the unique landscapes of this beautiful island.
